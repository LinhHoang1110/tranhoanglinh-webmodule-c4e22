Personal Types

The Mediator

- You are a sensitive idealist motivated by your deeper personal values. You see the potential of a better future, and pursue truth and meaning with your own particular flair. You love motivating others to become better

- Job best suited: Graphics Designer, Pycologist, Writer, Artist, Editor, Therapist, HR Deverlopment manager, Councillor

The Protagonist

- You are people living and driven, with lots of enegy and a desire to express yourself. You always seem to be tuned into the needs of others, but remain optimistic and forward-looking

- Job best suited: Advertising executive, public Relations specialist, Corporate Coach, personal Trainer, Teacher, Motivation Speaker, headhunter, Fashion Design

The Provider

- Motivated to help others, you find yourself always attracted to roles and duties that benefit others. With family and friends your top priority in life, you are generous with your time, effort and emotions

- Job best suited: Sale Rep, Doctor/nurse, Life Coach, Loan Officer, Policeman/woman, Social Worker, Government Worker, Paramedic
